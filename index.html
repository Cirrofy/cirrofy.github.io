❖ Summary ❖

Yo, I'm Cirro (or digi_fav)! A mediocre digital artist that is too afraid to post his works online. I'm currently an IT student that has little to no experience on coding, but I'm pretty good at math. I have a Vtuber model but currently the model is unused yet. I used to draw with fingers and phone, but now I'm using a pen tablet. I'm multifandom: Fire Emblem, Vocaloid, Project Sekai, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and Furry. Also a former Pokémon fan. Indonesian. Besides drawing, I have experience on making fanmerch, such as keychains and totebags. I'm one of the founder of the circle "Milky Caffeine". This website purpose is for me to post my drawings outside of social media. Feel free to send me any message!
